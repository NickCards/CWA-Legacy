CWA LEGACY

Roadmap

The goal of CWA Legacy is to provide a stable, persistent experience for the community — a place where players can hang out, socialize, have fun, and help contribute ideas that improve the experience for everyone.

As development continues, our focus will remain on preserving player progression while expanding the game's functionality through new features, bug fixes, and community-driven improvements.

Community Events

Through events we hope to bridge the gap between missing features and player progression. We can reward titles that were formerly locked behind minigames that aren't yet added, and give out gear that was not formerly available to purchase in the shop.

We also plan on changing store pricing. With limited in-game progression currently, we plan on increasing the price of all items and gear. This will encourage players to grind — hopefully increasing enjoyment and longevity — and help keep inventories small at first to reduce load on the database. There will also be "flexy" items in the store costing an absurd amount of credits, so players can show off their dedication and grind!

Seasonal Store Rotations

Planned
  • Seasonal gear available in the store based on the time of year.
  • Holiday-themed items and rewards.

Leaderboard Competitions

Planned
  • Rewards for top players on minigame leaderboards.
  • Community score challenges and special events.
  • Potential seasonal and monthly competitions.

Major Features

Housing & Lots

In Progress

One of the most requested features for CWA Legacy — mainly by the friends who have been testing it for the last few months. Planned functionality includes:

  • Lot ownership and assignment.
  • House visitation support.
  • Friend-based house access.
  • Public housing listings within the housing menu.
  • Persistent housing data between server updates and restarts.

Card Commander & Card Assault

Planned

Some of my favorite games outside of Republic Defender, and high on the list of features to investigate. Before development begins, both games will be evaluated to determine which is more realistic to implement and maintain. Potential additions include:

  • Card pack purchases through the store.
  • Card collection storage in player inventories.
  • Persistent card progression and unlocks.
  • Competitive play.

Minor Features

Store / Marketplace

In Progress

Player Profiles

Planned

Expanding player profiles to better showcase progression and accomplishments:

  • Integration with housing and lot ownership.
  • High score and leaderboard displays.
  • Friends list integration.
  • Quizzes.
  • Displaying character customization and equipped gear.
  • Bug fixes related to online/offline status and data persistence.

Friends List

Planned
  • Player teleportation support.
  • Player profile pictures.
  • Integration with player profiles.
  • Traditional friend request approval system.
  • Player blocking functionality.

Player Titles

Planned
  • Adding all available player titles.
  • Unlocking titles through minigame stage completion.
  • Creating new methods of earning titles when original game systems are unavailable or incomplete, through community events.

Development priorities may change based on technical limitations, player feedback, and discoveries made along the way.
